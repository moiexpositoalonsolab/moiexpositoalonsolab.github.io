---
title: 'Town hall 2021: pool sequencing is delivering'
date: 2021-07-15
permalink: /posts/2021-07-15/
---

At our July 2021 general meeting, the network came together to share encouraging progress. After the first seasons of sampling, pool sequencing was producing good results, and many of the GrENE-net samples were already moving through the bioinformatics pipeline.

A highlight was our cost-effective sequencing protocol, which lets us process thousands of plants affordably — making an experiment of this scale feasible. We also discussed efficient methods for analysing pooled sequences, early signals of rapid evolution across sites, and the future of the experiment.
